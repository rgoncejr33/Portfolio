Get To Know Me

Hello, my name is Ricardo Gonce Jr and the two most important titles I will ever hold are father and husband. Growing up in the South Bronx allowed me to develop a thick skin, the ability to observe and read a room as well as having this never-ending thirst for knowledge and success. Since a young man I've always carried two lessons my grandfather taught me: the first was that as a man my word must be bond, if I say I'm going to do something; then I have to follow through on that promise. The second is work ethic, ill never be the fastest, or smartest but I had to be willing to outwork every and anybody. The first one in and the last one out was what my Papa lived by and something that I try to live by every day. I uphold this promise to him by always having a positive attitude and always giving max effort.

Why I became a developer? It's kind of a long story but the short version is after being affected by a mass layoff I found myself jobless and feeling like I had nothing to offer. I started my journey as a self-taught developer by taking a few classes on LinkedIn learning about HTML and CSS. I enjoyed it so much that I enrolled in a 4 week Web Development Fundamentals Bootcamp at Nucamp. I excelled at Nucamp and most importantly I regained my smile and sense of worth. I was finally coding. I tried learning multiple times over the years but for some reason or another, I never had the drive to persevere. This time was different I was doing this for me, to prove I was capable and I was smart and I could be a developer. I finished Nucamp's Bootcamp 4th in my class with a 98.81%. I now am enrolled at 100Devs 30-week Software Engineering Bootcamp, I will be graduating in April of 2023 as a Full-Stack Developer.
